Why I want to become a developer?

I can understand why someone might want to become a developer. Many people choose this career path because they have a passion for technology and enjoy using their skills to solve problems and build new things. There's a certain satisfaction that comes with being able to take an idea and turn it into a functioning piece of software or app. Additionally, the field of software development is growing rapidly, and there are plenty of opportunities for career advancement and financial stability. Overall, becoming a developer can be a rewarding and fulfilling choice for those who are interested in technology and problem-solving.
